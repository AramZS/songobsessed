Sune Rose Wagner is a Danish musician and songwriter living in New York. Most acclaimed worldwide is his work in the band The Raveonettes. He was born in 1973. He recently released his first solo album with Danish lyrics, due to an intermezzo in The Raveonettes during Sharon Foos maternity leave. His first band was called Western Front which later changed name to Psyched Up Janis and become very popular in Denmark. He has since worked with Danish bands such as Sort Sol and Viva Vertigo.
